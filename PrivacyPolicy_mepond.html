Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how mepond ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Our core privacy principle: We only access your device's GPS location to provide map functionality within the app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information beyond what is absolutely necessary for the app to function.

How We Use Location Information

The GPS location accessed by our app is used exclusively for:

Core Functionality

Displaying your current position on the map within the application.

Local Processing

All location processing occurs directly on your device. No location data is transmitted to any external servers.

No Persistent Storage

Your location information is not stored on your device beyond your current app session. When you close the app, this information is discarded.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your location data or any other information with any third parties, organizations, or individuals.

Since we do not collect or transmit any data from your device, there is no information to share with external entities.

Your Control and Choices

Location Permissions

You have full control over location access:

  • You can grant or revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings
  • The app will only access location when you are actively using it
  • You can use your device's location settings to provide approximate rather than precise location

Opting Out

If you prefer not to share your location, you can:

  • Deny location permission when prompted
  • Disable location services for this app in your device settings
  • Use the app without location features (map will display without your position)

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
